Important Information

If you have found a stray, injured, or abandoned cat in urgent need of help, please contact our emergency line or a local animal rescue service immediately. Do not use the contact form below for urgent rescue situations, as responses may take time.

Our contact form is for general enquiries only, including adoption questions, volunteering, or partnerships. For updates about a specific cat or adoption application, please check your email or contact us directly—do not submit multiple form requests. We aim to respond to all enquiries as quickly as possible and appreciate your patience while our team reviews your message.
